Title: Push Down Pumping
Push down pumping builds arm strength and promotes lymph drainage by engaging the shoulder and upper back.

Instructions:
1. Sit or stand comfortably.
2. Stretch your arm overhead, open your fingers.
3. Make a fist while pushing down with arms, squeezing shoulder blades together. Inhale and tighten muscles.
4. Stretch arms back overhead while exhaling and relaxing.
Repeat 4 times.
