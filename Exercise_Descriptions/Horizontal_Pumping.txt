Title: Horizontal Pumping
Horizontal pumping promotes lymph drainage through outward horizontal arm movements and resistance-based muscle tightening.

Instructions:
1. Sit or stand comfortably.
2. Close your fingers into fists while drawing arms in front of your chest. Inhale and tighten muscles.
3. Open your fingers while extending arms horizontally to shoulder height. Exhale and relax muscles.
Repeat 4 times.
